**C4 (Constitutional amendment barrier)** — Addressed. The conclusion now explicitly states that changing chamber sizes to achieve NestSection compatibility requires a constitutional amendment in most states, not merely a statutory change. The language has been appropriately qualified.

**C2 (Iowa statutory vs. constitutional nesting)** — Addressed with the M3 footnote noting that Iowa's nesting requirement is statutory (Iowa Code § 42.4) rather than constitutional, making amendment easier.
