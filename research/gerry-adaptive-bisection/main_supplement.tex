\begin{abstract}
This document provides supplementary materials for ``Edge-Weighting Makes Method Selection Irrelevant: Complete Equivalence of Recursive and N-Way Partitioning for VRA Compliance.'' It includes: (A) algorithmic fairness framework and redistricting implications, (B) advanced theoretical results including smoothed analysis and robustness to perturbations, (C) detailed spatial structure analysis with clustering metrics, (D) comprehensive compactness comparison to enacted 2020 congressional plans, (E) extended discussion of fairness guarantees and gaming resistance, and (F) complete experimental protocol details.
\end{abstract}
