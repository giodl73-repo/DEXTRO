## Notes

- **Geographic sorting formula**: Pearson correlation between tract-level D vote% and population density
- **Partisan bias formula**: (D seat%) - (Statewide D vote%), expressed in percentage points
- **Urban threshold**: Tracts with >1000 people/sq mi classified as urban
- **CRS for area calculations**: EPSG:5070 (NAD83 / Conus Albers) for accurate area in continental US
