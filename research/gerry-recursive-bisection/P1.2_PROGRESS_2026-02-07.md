### Geographic Constraint Hypothesis

Alabama's Black population (26.5% statewide, concentrated in Black Belt region) may be:
1. **Too dispersed** to create compact 50%+ districts
2. **Too concentrated** to create multiple MM districts without extreme gerrymandering
3. **Geographically incompatible** with compactness + population balance constraints

**Enacted Strategy**: Pack 53% Black population into 2 districts (supermajorities)
**Algorithmic Result**: Distribute 30-36% Black population across 5 districts (no majorities)
