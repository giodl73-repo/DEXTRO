\begin{abstract}
This document provides supplementary materials for ``Quantifying the Voting Rights Act-Compactness Tradeoff: Non-Majority-Minority Districts Generally Benefit from Demographic-Aware Redistricting.'' It includes: (A) complete technical implementation details with pseudocode and METIS parameters, (B) extended methodology covering all metrics and statistical procedures, (C) additional results including urban-rural analysis, enacted plans comparison, and South Carolina infeasibility investigation, (D) ensemble validation with ReCom comparison, and (E) additional figures and tables referenced in the main text.
\end{abstract}
