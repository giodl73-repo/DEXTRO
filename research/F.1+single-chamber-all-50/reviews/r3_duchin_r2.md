**C2 (PP inflation decomposition)** — Addressed. Section 4.1 now includes the sentence directing readers to F.5 for the decomposition of the compactness advantage into scale and resolution components. The specific figures from F.5 (+0.027 scale effect, +0.013 resolution effect) are cross-referenced. This prevents readers from misattributing the full PP advantage to algorithm quality.

**C4 (Compactness comparison confound)** — Addressed via cross-reference. The note added to Section 4.1 ("the higher PP at state house scale relative to congressional scale is primarily a scale effect, not evidence of superior algorithmic performance — see F.5 for decomposition") is exactly what I requested.
