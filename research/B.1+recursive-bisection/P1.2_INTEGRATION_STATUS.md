## Architecture Overview

```
Pipeline Flow (VRA Mode):

1. run_state_redistricting.py --partition-mode metis-vra
   ↓
2. Load tract geometries + demographics
   ↓
3. vra_utils.create_vra_vertex_weights()
   → Returns: np.array (n_tracts, 2)
     - Column 0: Total population
     - Column 1: Minority VAP
   ↓
4. vra_utils.calculate_vra_target_weights(target_mm=2)
   → Returns: [[0.143, 0.213], [0.143, 0.213], ...]
     - 2 districts with high minority concentration
     - 5 districts with low minority concentration
   ↓
5. RecursiveBisection(vra_mode=True, vra_target_weights=...)
   ↓
6. partition_graph(..., multi_constraint=True)
   ↓
7. METIS balances both constraints:
   - Population ±1% (constitutional requirement)
   - Minority VAP concentrated in target districts
   ↓
8. Output: Districts with 1-2 MM districts (50%+ minority)
```
