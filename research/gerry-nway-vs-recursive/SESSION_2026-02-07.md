## Key Insights from Today

### 1. Coalition Districts Are Essential

**Alabama Example**:
- **Black-only (25.6%)**: 0/2 MM districts ❌ (max 41.6%)
- **Total minority (36.9%)**: 2/2 MM districts ✓ (max 50.8%)

**California Example**:
- **Hispanic-only (39.4%)**: 17/20 MM districts ⚠️ (85%)
- **Total minority (65.3%)**: 43/33 MM districts ✓ (130%)

**Takeaway**: Using total minority (all non-White) is critical for VRA compliance in diverse states.

### 2. Edge-Weighting Beats Multi-Constraint

**Multi-constraint (tpwgts)**: Constraint conflict → poor results
**Edge-weighting**: Clean optimization → excellent results

**Weight factor 5x @ threshold 0.40**: Most consistent success across states

### 3. Papers Build on Each Other

**P1 (Recursive Bisection)**:
- Introduces algorithm
- Claims structural immunity to partisan manipulation
- References P2 for VRA analysis

**P2 (VRA Compliance)**:

**Together**: Demonstrate algorithmic redistricting can be both partisan-neutral AND VRA-compliant.
